Welcome

Hey! Welcome to my site, replacing my wordpress one (still available but won’t be updated with new posts - they’ll be here instead!)

The purpose of this site is so I can share breakdowns & tutorials about various shader effects I’ve made & shared on my twitter, @Cyanilux. I use Unity3D, with Universal Render Pipeline and usually Shader Graph, but the concepts might still translate well to other engines and forms of shader creation.

The tutorials here are all completely free to read and mainly come in two forms :

  • “Posts” which go over general shader topics or explaining shadergraph nodes
  • “Breakdowns” which break down creating a specific shader effect.

Images in all posts can be clicked to show a fullscreen version.

Check out the Contents or Recent Pages in the Navigation bar above to start reading/learning!

~ Cyan
